Music
===========================================
/bassboost
Toggles the bassboost audio filter on/off

/filter
Manage audio filters

/pitch
Change the pitch of the song

/speed
Change the speed of the song

/autoplay
Toggle auto play mode (Play recommended music)

/debug
Return some debug information. (useful for support)

/fastforward
Fast forwards the player by your specified amount; The default is 10 seconds

/grab
Send the current music information to your dm.

/join
Makes the bot join your voice channel

/jump
Jump to the specified track in the queue

/leavecleanup
Remove song(s) added by members who have left the voice channel

/loop
Toggle loop song

/lyrics
Get song lyrics from the current playing music.


/music-panel
Get link of the online music panel

/nowplaying
Show which music is currently playing in the voice channel with a beautiful image.

/pause
Pause music

/play-file
Play a song from a file


/play
Play a song in a voice channel from a link or a search query.

/previous
Play the previous song in the queue.

/queue
Shows the queue

/removedupes
Remove duplicate song in the queue

/resume
Resume the player if it is paused.

/rewind
Rewind the player by your specified amount; The default is 10 seconds

/seek
Skips to the specified timestamp in the currently playing track format: H:M:S

/shuffle
Shuffles the queue

/skip
Skip current song and start the next one or start a vote to skip

/top-songs
Show most played songs on [Nama Bot] with a beautiful image.

/tts
Text to speach in voice channel

/volume
Change [Nama Bot]'s audio volume

/24-7
Toggle 24/7 mode for the bot. If enabled, the bot will stay in the voice channel forever.

/announcechannel
Set the channel where the music is announced

/controller
Setup controller

/defaultplaylist
Set the default playlist

/djmode
Configure DJ Mode for music commands

/moveannouncechannel
Move the channel where the music is announced

/setvc
Restrict the bot to only join specific voice channels.

/pl-add-queue
Add current queue tracks to your playlist

/pl-add-song
Add songs to your playlist

/pl-create
Create a [Nama Bot] playlist

/pl-delete
Delete a [Nama Bot] playlist

/pl-list
List your [Nama Bot] playlists

/pl-play
Play your [Nama Bot] playlist

/pl-remove-song
Remove a song in your [Nama Bot] playlist

/pl-shuffle
Shuffle your playlist

/pl-spotify
See your Spotify playlists

/pl-view
View your [Nama Bot] playlists

/pl-visibility
Change the visibility of a playlist

/check-vote
Show if you have voted or not.

/clearqueue
Clear the queue.

/disconnect
Delete queue and disconnect the bot from voice channel

/fix
Change the voice region of the voice channel to fix the connection issue.

/history
Show the last songs you listened to.

/move
Move song in the queue

/music-guesser
Music guesser game

/music-info
Searches for and displays information about a song.

/music-logs
See the logs of music actions.

/radios
Radios system of [Nama Bot]

/remove
Remove a song from the queue

/spotify
Spotify integration

Start an interactive music panel to easily play music by genre
